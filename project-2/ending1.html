The soldiers grab you, and ask you to come with them. You twist in their grasp, trying to break free. They push you into a group with other students, and throw the professor in after you.

"We only want to ask you all a few quick questions," the soldiers announce.

You can tell by the smug sneers on their faces that they're lying. You wonder what your parents will think when they hear the news. As reality settles in, you can have no choice but to look ahead toward the uncertain future.

This is only one potential truth.
