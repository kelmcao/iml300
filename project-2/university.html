When you arrive at school, the situation isn't much better. A crowd of student rioters has formed, some stoning the windows, holding chairs, pipes, and other common tools. They demand the expulsion of the PKI, and you watch as a teacher is pushed outside by soldiers, bruised and beaten. The students leap on her, demanding to be repaid for the deaths of the six generals committed by her party. Not far, you spy a soldier watching them, occasionally whispering directions to students. Out of the corner of your eye, you spot a man being pulled out of an alleyway by a few soldiers. He seems resistant, and you want to see what is happening.
